OneSearch by Hoxly

Much of our online lives is locked behind virtual walls that require a username and password to access. With OneSearch we are breaking down those walls allowing you to search through your online accounts the same way you search through everything else, with a search engine like Google or Bing. With the OneSearch browser extension you can sign into your various cloud accounts like OneDrive, Google Drive/Docs, and Facebook, and receive personalized results right on the search results page of your favorite search engine.
